The Forge of Civilization
Every station in the galaxy runs on a chain of extraction, refinement, and assembly. Miners haul raw ores from asteroids at a 1:7 smelting ratio — one chunk of ore yields seven ingots. Those ingots combine into alloys and wiring, which are then assembled into the 20 components that keep your colony alive: circuit boards for your AI systems, solar cells for power, life support for citizens, and reactor cores for the most ambitious builds.

Hover any element to trace its ingredient chain. Click to add it to your build queue below — the calculator figures out everything you need to mine, smelt, and assemble.

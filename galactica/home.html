SYS // StationConstruction

Design your station

Place modules on a crossword-puzzle grid that expands in every direction. Crew quarters go inside walls. Docking bays face outward. Enclose empty cells to create storage bays that hold 64 units each. Foundries sit between bays as directional membranes — ore goes in one side, alloy comes out the other.

SYS // IndustryProduction

Manufacture at scale

Smelt iron ore into ingots. Combine ingots with carbon to forge steel. Press steel and titanium alloy into reactor cores and shield emitters. Four production tiers, 40+ recipes, and your citizens operate the foundries — hauling fuel, feeding inputs, collecting outputs. Set a foundry to run indefinitely and it will, as long as someone keeps it fed.

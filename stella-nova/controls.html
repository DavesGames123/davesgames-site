TIPS // Worth Knowing
⎋
ESC peels one layer. Selection → journal → station mgmt → pause.
⊕
Zoom follows the cursor. Point at something far away, scroll, fly to it.
▦
Close zoom = pick modules. Zoom out and the whole station selects.
+
Add to selection with Ctrl/⌘ + click or drag.
▭
Drag picks smart. Ships beat stations beat planets beat asteroids.
◑
Right-click is contextual. Module info, cancel build, or pan (drag).
↻
R cycles stations and opens station management.
▷
Free-flight piloting: W thrust, ←/→ rotate.

The Forge of Civilization
Every station runs on a chain of extraction, refinement, and assembly. Miners haul raw ores from asteroids at a 1:1 smelting ratio — one chunk of ore yields one ingot in a Foundry. Ingots combine into alloys in Assemblers, then assemble into 20 components in Component Labs through a deeply interconnected dependency web. Layer 1 components feed into Layer 2, which feed into Layer 3 pinnacle items — the Navigation Unit and Reactor Core that anchor your Colony Core.

Building a Station Core requires ~79 ore. A Colony Core demands ~386 ore flowing through every tier of the production chain.

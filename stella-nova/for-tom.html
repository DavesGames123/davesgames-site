Standing Orders Three Design Pillars

Authoritative · Per Steam Brief
SYS NOMINAL SECT 01

Pillar A●

PILLAR · TACTICS

Tactics, not reflexes

Bullets matter, movement is tuned for decisions, and the tension is claustrophobic, not chaotic. Fights reward positioning, patience, and smart use of the environment over raw reflexes.

Pillar B●

PILLAR · ROLE

Engineer. Soldier. Survivor.

You're not a red shirt. You're a mechanically inclined (but battle-trained) engineer — and the only one who can keep the ship running.

Pillar C●

PILLAR · ENVIRONMENT

Use the environment to your advantage

Weaponize the ship. Trigger hazards and environmental kills — but watch out, because stray bullets from enemy robots can cause problems for you, too.

Tactical Pause v2 SUPERHOT-Like Time Dilation

In Implementation
DILATION SPEED v2 PARTIAL

Doctrine●

SCREENSHOT · TIME-DILATED MOMENT

"Starts very slow motion, but speeds up to about 70% when you decide to act."

Time dilates around player intent. At rest the simulation runs in heavy slow-motion; the moment the player commits to an action — moving, shooting, interacting with a device, or casting an ability — time accelerates to roughly 70% real-speed.

Net effect: planning happens almost-paused; commitment costs you tempo. Action and analysis share the same control surface.

Defense Mode

Live out the AstroTechs story. Repair damaged systems and complete objectives. Solve puzzles, overcome powerful enemies, speak with fellow AstroTechs to unravel the mystery of why the ship has been rerouted from its original mission, and rescue crew members to bring them to safety.

Horde Mode

Survive escalating waves as you open new areas, activate traps, and upgrade your arsenal. Combat-first; no narrative beats.

The Elevator Pitch

30 Seconds

Stella Nova is a space colony simulation where you mine asteroids, smelt ore into alloys, build modular stations piece by piece, and govern AI citizens who form relationships, hold grudges, and run your foundries autonomously. It's built on a custom Rust engine with real N-body orbital physics, and it's the first game in the genre to use LLM-powered narrative AI — meaning every colonist interaction generates unique, emergent stories that no two players will ever share.

One-line pitch: "Factorio meets RimWorld in space — with AI that writes the stories."

Technical Differentiators

Why This Can't Be Cloned Easily

  • Custom Rust engine ("quarkstrom"): Purpose-built with wgpu. No Unity/Unreal overhead. Thousands of physics bodies at 60fps. MOAT
  • Barnes-Hut N-body gravity: Real orbital mechanics, not on-rails. Asteroids, stations, and debris interact gravitationally. MOAT
  • LLM-powered narrative system: First colony sim with generative AI storytelling. Citizens don't just have stats — they have conversations, grudges, and evolving relationships driven by language models. FIRST MOVER
  • HDR rendering pipeline: Rgba16Float → bloom → tonemap → CRT blit. Retro aesthetic with modern rendering tech.
  • Modular station construction: Crossword-puzzle placement rules. Emergent station designs, not prefabs.
